(101955) Bennu

Type: B-type asteroid
Closest Approach to Earth: ~300,000 km (within lunar distance)
Orbital Period: 436.6 days (~1.2 years)
Description: Bennu is famous for being the target of NASA's OSIRIS-REx

close approaches make it a Potentially Hazardous Asteroid (PHA).
(99942) Apophis

Type: S-type asteroid
Closest Approach to Earth: ~31,600 km (approaching Earth in 2029)
Orbital Period: 323 days (~0.88 years)
Description: Apophis is notable for its extremely close approach to Earth in
2029, within the distance of geostationary satellites. It was once thought
to pose a risk of collision with Earth.

Potentially Hazardous Asteroids (PHAs)

PHAs are asteroids with orbits that bring them very close to Earth,
typically within 7.5 million km, and have a size large enough (140 meters or more)
to cause significant damage in the event of an impact.
